Bookmark to Keep

Your important posts are safe. The script automatically identifies and skips any tweet you've bookmarked, giving you complete control.

Total Privacy

Detweeter runs entirely on your computer. Your credentials are only used to log in locally and are never stored or sent anywhere.

Free & Open Source

Built with Python and Selenium, the entire project is transparent. You can inspect the code yourself on GitHub. Forget costly API-based services.

Simple as 1-2-3

1

Download & Run

No complex installation. Just download the executable and launch the app.

2

Enter Credentials

Provide your handle and password so the app can log in on your behalf, just like you would.

3

Choose & Delete

Select a deletion mode (all or the last 'N' tweets) and watch it go to work in real-time.
